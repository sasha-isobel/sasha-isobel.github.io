My name is Sasha and I love the outdoors! I go camping every summer with my family and friends. My favourite things to do while camping are swimming in the lake, hiking and canoeing/kayaking. Also, I used to do Girl Guides where we went on lots of cool sleepover trips and I learned many outdoor skills! Lastly, I like watching shows such as Survivor and Survivorman because they're in the nature.
Here are a few my favourite provincial/national parks that I've visited:

  • Bon Echo - Ontario Provincial Park
  • Killbear - Ontario Provincial Park
  • Thingvellir - Icelandic National Park
  • Silent Lake - Ontario Provincial Park
